Sprint Retrospective

Week 6 Sprint Retrospective

What went well?

This week, I made progress on making the hi-fi prototype, ensuring that the key user flows are visually consistent and smoothly functioned. I refined and expanded on the existing pages, such as notifications and widgets, and continued to align the UI with the youthful, quirky brand identity I developed earlier.

What didn't go well?

While refining the hi-fi prototype, I sometimes got caught up in the small design details like tweaking spacing, colours, and layouts which slowed down my progress more than expected. Also I realised that some of the layout may not look that intuitive and needed to be refined further.

What can be improved?

The use of my illustration could be improved as now it is just acting as a design element but instead I could use the different characters in different area, for example, one character as the welcoming character and others can act different positions.

What actions will I take for the next sprint?

  • Finalise and polish the hi-fi prototype based on the WIP feedback
  • Draft a clear usability testing plan and recruit participants
  • Focus on capturing user feedback on core flows rather than visual design details
