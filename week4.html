Sprint Retrospective

Week 4 Sprint Retrospective

What went well?

This week, I conducted usability testing for my lo-fi wireframes and gathered clear, actionable feedback. I also created a moodboard, started developing brand identity elements, and worked on the design strategy document. Completing the pitch presentation and receiving feedback gave me clarity and direction for moving forward. I felt productive and confident, especially with the validation I received from both user testing and lecturer input.

What didn't go well?

I occasionally felt overwhelmed balancing multiple deliverablesâ€”slides, strategy document, and prototype updates. While I did manage to complete the tasks, there was some uncertainty about what to prioritise next. At times, I lacked confidence in whether I was moving in the right direction.

What can be improved?

I've learned that breaking large tasks into smaller, prioritised steps can help reduce stress and improve focus. I also realised that consistent check-ins (with peers and lecturers) helped me stay aligned and confident in my direction.

What actions will I take for the next sprint?

  • Begin transitioning lo-fi wireframes into hi-fi prototypes
  • Start creating brand identity and stylescape
  • Keep brand direction consistent across all design outputs
  • Test hi-fi prototypes for further iteration
